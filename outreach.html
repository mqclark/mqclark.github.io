Outreach + Creative Work

Taking neuroscience out of the lab.

A lot of good science happens when more people get their hands on it: students, teachers, and communities who don't usually have a research lab down the hall.

Pueblo Brain Science

Hands-on neuroscience, built as a partnership.

Pueblo Brain Science runs hands-on neuroscience workshops with Indigenous students and educators in New Mexico, and I help lead it. Each workshop sets up stations where students work directly with flies and simple experiments, the same kind of hands-on science my Bucknell students do.

It's a partnership, not a drop-in visit: the communities we work with shape what the workshops become.
